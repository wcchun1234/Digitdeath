Background info:

The 5th wave of pandemic of COVID-19 in HK caused a huge number of deaths in HK. The public health system was overloaded, which caused them before dying, they need to live in an overclouded hospital. With a poor living environment, and never can arrange to meet with their relatives or families.

The whole process is very upsetting. Even we can feel their helplessness from the news pictures taken in the hospital at that time.

This work is helping to create a platform mentally having a “gathering venue”, creating their unique profile picture, live forever in this “home”. Reminding the people in the future, and letting us have a chance to worship them.
